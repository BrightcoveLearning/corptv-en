Publish/Unpublish
  1. Publish/Unpublish the page using the button at the top of navigation panel.
Edit page details

  1. Click to edit the page details including:

    • Change page title and title translations
    • Control page availability, including calendar, device, and location restrictions
    • Archive the page
Change page layout
  1. Hover over the current layout.
  2. Click Change to choose a new layout.
Add playlist(s)
  1. Click Add Playlist to add one or more the playlist(s) for display on the page.
Replace/Remove a playlist
  1. Click the ellipses (...) next to the playlist.
  2. Choose Replace Playlist or Remove Playlist.
Change playlist layout
  1. Click the ellipses (...) next to the playlist.
  2. In the dialog that opens, click Layout options.
  3. Choose between Landscape or Portrait mode for the thumbnails.
Alter availability

The Availability settings are used to restrict page availability based upon:

  • Schedule
  • Devices
  • Locations
  1. Click to the right of the Availability label, or the setting you wish to change.

View linked page

If you are using linked pages with one or more of your playlists you can view your linked page from here.

  1. Click the Linked page link which appears under the playlist name with which the linked pages is associated.
