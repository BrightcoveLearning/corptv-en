Create new page

Details

Duplicate page
  1. Click next to page name.
Edit page layout and content
  1. To edit how the page looks and the content which is displayed, click on the page name itself to be taken to the page details.
Edit page settings
  1. To edit the page settings, including changing the title and availability, hover over a page name and click . This opens the Page Settings dialog.
Archive a page
  1. Hover over a page name and click . This opens the Page Settings dialog.
  2. On the bottom-left of the page settings dialog, click Archive page. The page is immediately archived and you are returned to the index page of the Layout module with the archived page no longer in the page list.

Details

Restore or delete an archived page

Once a page has been archived, you can either restore it to your page list or permanently delete it.

  1. At the bottom of the page list in the navigation panel, click . The Archived Pages panel opens.
  2. Click to restore the archived page.
  3. Click to permanently delete the page.
Change page title
  1. Hover over a page name and click . This opens the Page Settings dialog.
  2. In the Title and Translations section, you can change the default page title and the title for other enabled languages.
Change page order

  1. To change the order of pages, click on a page name and drag it to a new position.

Preview app layout by device
  1. Click a device icon at the top of the page
