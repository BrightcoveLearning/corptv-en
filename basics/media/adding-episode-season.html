How To: Add an Episode to a Season

Steps

  1. In the primary navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All series.
  3. Click on a series title to open the series details.
  4. On the series Overview page, click on the season to add episodes to.
  5. Click Add Episode.
  6. Click on a video to select it add and click Select Video.

All videos added to a season will be displayed on the right side of the series Overview page.
