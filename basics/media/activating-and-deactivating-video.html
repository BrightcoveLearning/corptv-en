How To: Activate and Deactivate a Video

Steps

  1. Click Media in the primary navigation.
  2. The Status column indicates if a video is activated or deactivated.
    • - Indicates the video is active
    • - Indicates the video is inactive (can't be played)
  3. To Activate a video, select it and then click More > Activate.
  4. To Deactivate a video, select it and then click More > Deactivate.

If the Status column is not displayed, use the column selector to select it.
