How To: Edit Basic Video Details

Steps

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. Click on a video title to open the video details page.
  3. The video details are grouped into tabs with each tab having one or more sections. Click Edit or double-click in a section. Some sections allow the details to be edited directly.
  4. Click Save to save the changes. Some sections do not have a save button and will autosave when tabbing out of the field.
