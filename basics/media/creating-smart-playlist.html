How To: Create a Video Cloud Smart Playlist

Steps

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. In the panel navigation, click the Create playlist link.
  3. Enter a Playlist name.
  4. For the Playlist type, click on Video Cloud smart playlist.
  5. Click Create playlist. The panel will close and re-open.
  6. Select a Parameter of Tags.
  7. Set the operator to Match any.
  8. Enter the appropriate tag(s) and press enter.
  9. Click Apply.
  10. Click X to close the panel.
  11. Click the playlist name just created in the panel navigation panel and confirm the correct videos have been added to the playlist.
