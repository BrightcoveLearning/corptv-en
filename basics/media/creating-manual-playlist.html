How To: Create a Video Cloud Manual Playlist

Steps

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. In the panel navigation, click the Create playlist link.
  3. Enter a Playlist name.
  4. For the Playlist type, click Video Cloud manual playlist.
  5. Click Create playlist. The panel will close and re-open.
  6. Check Show Playlist in Sidebar to have the playlist appear in the sidebar.
  7. Click X to close the panel.
  8. To add videos to the playlist, click All videos in the panel navigation to display all the videos in the account.
  9. Click the checkbox next to videos you want to add to the playlist.
  10. Click More > Add To Playlist.
  11. Select a playlist and then click Add.

To rearrange the order of the videos, select the playlist and then click on a row and drag it to a new position in the list.
